We are Trinity Prep’s Student Investment Fund

Founded in 2025, TPSIF is a student-run organization dedicated to hands-on investing education and portfolio management.

Our Pillars

Education

Weekly workshops cover equity research, financial modeling, and market analysis led by senior analysts and industry speakers.

Portfolio Management

Members pitch ideas, undergo Q&A sessions, and vote on stocks and ETFs to include in our live fund, gaining real investment experience.

Professional Development

Through alumni mentorship, recruiting workshops, and our annual conference, TPSIF prepares members for internships and careers in finance.
