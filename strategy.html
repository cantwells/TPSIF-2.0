Investment Strategy

Our Philosophy

At TPSIF, we blend fundamental research with a long-term outlook. We seek companies with strong competitive advantages, solid balance sheets, and management teams aligned with shareholder interests.

Investment Process

  1. Idea Generation: Members source and pitch investment ideas based on quantitative screening and qualitative research.
  2. Due Diligence: In-depth analysis of financials, competitive landscape, and risk factors.
  3. Committee Review: Senior members evaluate pitches and vote on portfolio inclusion.
  4. Monitoring & Reporting: Ongoing performance tracking and quarterly reports to stakeholders and administration.
